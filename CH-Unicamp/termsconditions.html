CH-Unicamp

Expressive Visemes Images Database





What is CH-Unicamp?

CH-Unicamp is an annotated database of expressive visemes images played by a female face. In the context of CH-Unicamp, visemes (or visual phonemes) are photographic images of the face producing different phones of the Brazilian Portuguese language. The visemes of Brazilian Portuguese language are common to other languages. CH-Unicamp contains photographic samples of visemes extracted from speech accompanied by the expression of emotions. The database comprehends the twenty-two emotions of the Ortony, Clore and Collins (OCC) model of emotions. The structure of the database is described here.
