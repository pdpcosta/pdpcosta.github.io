DG-Unicamp

Driver Gaze Zone Dataset With Depth Data





What is DG-Unicamp?

DG-Unicamp is an annotated database of faces of drivers. The dataset contains images captured by a Intel Realsense R200 depth camera. It was formed by images captured from 19 points of interest on car front view. The images were collected from 4 different cameras (Color, Infrared 1, Infrared 2 and Depth) The structure of the database is described here.
